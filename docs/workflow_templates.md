# Workflow Templates

## Legal Matter Review

Flow:

```text
Matter Intake
-> Chronology Builder
-> Issue Spotting
-> Evidence Matrix
-> Legal Authority Mapping
-> Risk Analysis
-> Draft Legal Memo
-> Human Review
-> Audit Log
```

Outputs:

- Matter Profile;
- Timeline;
- Issue Map;
- Evidence Matrix;
- Missing Materials List;
- Draft Legal Memo;
- Review Log;
- Audit Pack JSON;
- Feedback Eval Dataset JSON.

Risk posture: do not state final legal conclusions. Present risk, evidence strength, assumptions, and missing facts.

## Compliance Impact Review

Flow:

```text
Regulation Intake
-> Obligation Extraction
-> Business Mapping
-> Gap Assessment
-> Risk Scoring
-> Remediation Plan
-> Approval Workflow
-> Audit Pack
```

Outputs:

- Regulation Summary;
- Obligation Register;
- Business Impact Map;
- Gap Analysis;
- Risk Register;
- Remediation Tracker;
- Compliance Impact Memo;
- Audit Pack JSON.

## Deal Due Diligence Memo

Flow:

```text
Deal Intake
-> VDR Indexing
-> Red Flag Screening
-> Contract Matrix
-> Evidence Mapping
-> Risk Materiality
-> Draft DD Memo
-> Human Review
-> Audit Log
```

Outputs:

- Deal Profile;
- VDR Index;
- Red Flag Dashboard;
- Contract Matrix;
- Evidence Panel;
- Diligence Question List;
- Draft DD Memo;
- Audit Pack JSON;
- Feedback Eval Dataset JSON.
